Eating

Eat as many calories as used each day to maintain. Center eating around protein sources. Eat a variety of ingredients, especially vegetables, to get enough micronutrients. If cooking at home like me, gather whole ingredients, and cook simple tasty meals, preferably over a fire. Also, drink lots of water all day.

I break up my eating into three big meals centered around protein.

  1. Smoothie for breakfast, drinking calories helps me gain weight
  2. Lunchbox/Salad for lunch
  3. Something off my menu for dinner
