FAQ

How do I pronounce your name?

Similar to “Roo-pwrecked”. I speak conversational German and I even I can’t pronounce it correctly. “-echt” is hard for English speakers, the English equivalent is Robert. Ruprecht comes from the Old High German words “hrothi” (meaning flame) and “bert” (meaning bright), which I think is pretty cool. Coincidentally, “Blake” is derived from Old English and either means “white, bright” or “black, dark”, which seems fitting for a computer scientist :)
