The Sword

Breathing is the sword that slashes thought.

There’s no way to figure it all out, so instead of trying to untanble the spaghetti loop of thinking, use breathing to slash it all up.

You will never stop the flow of thoughts.

That’s not the goal. The goal is training your attention muscle to refocus over and over and over. Focus on the breath – in and out. Once you lose it and start focusing on something else (e.g. your thoughts), return to focusing about the breath.

Meditation is the art of learning to 1) notice your mind is wandering and 2) bring it back to the present – any focus you choose.

At first, it’s breathing. But eventually, meditation can extend into every aspect of living. Cleaning dishes becomes meditation – just focus on the actions. Brushing teeth becomes meditation – focus on perfect form.
