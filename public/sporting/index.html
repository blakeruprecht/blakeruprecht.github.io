Sporting

  • Related

Sports are just fights/competitions with rules. Fighting is the oldest version of sporting where the first rule was created: no killing. From then on out, sportsmanship has been defined as playing fair according to the rules.

The voluntary limiting of arms. E.g. in soccer, you’re not allowed to stab or shoot anyone, even if you want to. Hell, you’re not even allowed to tackle! That’s what makes it a sport – there are rules for fair play.

Basically all sports combine running, lifting, throwing, and hitting.

  • fighting - oldest sport, first rules were “no killing”, e.g. pankration
  • hunting - individual sport, try to shoot prey
  • fishing - individual sport, try to lure and catch a fish
  • disc-golfing - individual sport, throw a disc at a target
  • balling - team sports involving a ball and a field of play
  • climbing - more of a form of traveling, but fun enough that it’s a sport

Related

  • running - can be a sport, training for other sports, or a form of traveling
  • lifting - can be a sport or training for other sports
  • gaming - hardly sports because they don’t use much holistic movement
