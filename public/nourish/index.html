Nourish

After breathing, movement, rest, and tools, water and food are the most important survival priorities. Humans can go about 3 days without water, and about 3 weeks without food. Gathering food has been the job of most humans who have ever lived – before roughly 10,000 BC, 100% of humans were hunters and/or gatherers. Since 10,000 BC, probably 95% of all humans who have ever lived were farmers.
